# Your Life in X ⏳

A personal project to visualize your life in **weeks, months or years** — and how many you have left.

Inspired by Tim Urban's [Your Life in Weeks](https://waitbutwhy.com/2014/05/life-weeks.html).

## 💡 What it does

- Enter your date of birth and the app calculates how many weeks, months or years you have lived
- Displays a visual grid where each cell represents a unit of time — filled cells are time already lived
- Shows a life progress bar with your current age percentage
- Displays a random quote about life and time on each load

---
